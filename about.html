About Me

Hi, I'm Nick Rackley, and I'm passionate about helping people unlock their creative potential with AI. I single-handily developed a leading AI-language learning app called Glyphz in one month, primarily by using chatGPT to substitute for a team of engineers. I then marketed it using primarily AI generated image and video ads. So many of my friends were shocked that an Army guy who didnt know how to code could transform their skillset so quickly, and asked me how I did it, so I decided to create this program.

I hold a MBA from Yale and a Masters Degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Washington. Before learning to code, I worked as a strategy consultant and did 8 years in the U.S. Army. I spend all of my time not coding or helping people code with my wonderful wife and two kids.

Trust me, if I can do this, you can too - let's transform your life together!
