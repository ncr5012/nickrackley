Delete Your Glyphz Account

To delete your account, please follow the steps below:

  1. Open the Glyphz app and click on the menu icon (three horizontal lines in the top right corner).
  2. From the menu options, select Delete Account.
  3. You will be prompted to confirm your decision. Click on Confirm to permanently delete your account.

Note: Once you confirm, all your account data will be permanently removed from our system and cannot be recovered.
