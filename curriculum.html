How to build AI Apps with AI Program

Embark on a journey to learn how to build AI applications using AI-powered tools. Below is an outline of what you'll learn:

  • Module 1: Introduction

    Introductory content to orient you to the course

    • 1.1: AI explained in 3 minutes
    • 1.2: Goals of this program, who its meant for
    • 1.3: Hacker principles

  • Module 2: Learn2Code project

    Starting with no skills other than being able to read, you will be able to build your own custom AI chatbot website in about 2 hours you can share with your friends

    • 2.1: Build a simple chatbot with Google Colab
    • 2.2: Customize your chatbot to be smarter than ChatGPT in a topic you care about (e.g., SAT-Prep)
    • 2.3: Deploy your custom chatbot to a website and share it with your friends

  • Module 3: Prototype an iOS/Android grocery app

    You will be able to snap a photo of your groceries, and the app will inform you which food it thinks may expire soon

    • 3.1: Setting Up React-Native (lets you code once for both iOS and Android)
    • 3.2: Build the prototype
    • 3.3: Test your prototype

  • Module 4: Going from prototype to App Store

    Learn how to build a great user interface for your app and meet Apple/Google requirements

    • 4.1: UI design
    • 4.2: App Store requirements in-app
    • 4.3: App Store requirements out-of-app (privacy policy, etc.)

  • Module 5: App Store review process

    Learn how to navigate the infamous App Store review process

    • 5.1: Apple review process
    • 5.2: Google Play review process
    • 5.3: Tips to make them happy

  • Module 6: Launching/marketing your app

    Get past the critical milestone of your first 100 users

    • 6.1: Demo your app in person
    • 6.2: Test ideas with Meta Ads
    • 6.3: Other marketing ideas
