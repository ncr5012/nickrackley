How to build AI Apps with AI Program

Embark on a journey to learn how to build AI applications using AI-powered tools. Below is an outline of what you'll learn:

  • Module 1: Introduction

    Introductory content to orient you to the course

    • 1.1: Who this course is for
    • 1.2: Hacker principles
    • 1.3: On programming languages/terms/professional developer stuff (spoiler: you don't need to care about any of this with AI)

  • Module 2: Learn2Code project

    Starting with no skills other than being able to read, you will be able to build your own custom AI chatbot website in about 2 hours you can share with your friends

    • Submodule 2.1: Build a simple chatbot with Google Colab
    • Submodule 2.2: Customize your chatbot to be smarter than chatGPT in a topic you care about (ex: SAT-Prep)
    • Submodule 2.3: Deploy your custom chatbot to a website and share it with your friends

  • Module 3: Prototype a iOS/android grocery app

    You will be able to snap a photo of your groceries and the app will inform you which food it thinks may expire soon

    • Submodule 3.1: Setting Up React-Native (lets you code once for both iOS and Android)
    • Submodule 3.2: Build the prototype
    • Submodule 3.3: Test your prototype

  • Module 4: Going from prototype to appstore

    Learn how to build a great user interface for your app and meet Apple/Google requirements

    • Submodule 4.1: UI design
    • Submodule 4.2: Appstore requirements in app
    • Submodule 4.3: Appstore requirements out of app (privacy policy, etc)
